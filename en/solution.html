Our Solution

Having acknowledged the magnitude and seriousness of the issue, the team has been brainstorming for a solution ever since last year, and after months of hard work, we present you The Glowing Tree. As mentioned in our introduction, The Glowing Tree is a hybrid social online education programme. There are several key terms in this name: "hybrid", "social", and "online". Therefore, it is essential for us to understand the definition of these terms.

"Hybrid"

A hybrid teaching programme means that it is composed by a non-profitable main part and a profitable counterpart. Just like the blue and red pill shown above, except the fact that you can take both of them in, and you will not be hunt down by Mr Smith because of your acknowledgement to the following information.

"Social"

As a social organisation, The Glowing Tree's ultimate goal is to mitigate the seemingly unchangeable problem of unequal distribution of education resources. Social responsibility is the core of this programme.

"Online"

Internet plays an exclusively essential role in the 21st century, to we recognise it as the best way to create real impact and brought opportunity to those who are desperately needed. Information about how certain barriers around the access to internet will be revealed in the following portion.

diagram

As shown above, The Glowing Tree incorporate two major components.

First, is a Free Online Education Website. Being the non-profitable part of the programme, the website will serve as a "Chinese version of the Khan Academy™️". The goal of the website, as introduced, is to provide opportunity to students that have no access to high school education and are longing for such. As mentioned in our research, such area has long been desolated, and needs to be addressed by proper solution. The website, at its initial stage, will provides three of the major courses in Chinese High School Curriculum. The team will also seise to cooperate with some of the most-established free online teaching platform for opportunity to reprint or translate the existed resources. By the same time, the team will developed a range of thorough learning structure referring to the traditional high school diploma and related skills that may be required. Then, the team will utilise the structure to sort the resources into the learning structure to create a range of learning plans, making it much more easy for students to follow.
