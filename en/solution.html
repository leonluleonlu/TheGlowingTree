Our Solution

Having acknowledged the magnitude and seriousness of the issue, the team has been brainstorming for a solution ever since last year, and after months of hard work, we present you The Glowing Tree. As mentioned in our introduction, The Glowing Tree is a hybrid social online education programme. There are several key terms in this name: "hybrid", "social", and "online". Therefore, it is essential for us to understand the definition of these terms. 

"Hybrid"

A hybrid teaching programme means that it is composed by a non-profitable main part and a profitable counterpart.

"Social"

As a social organisation, The Glowing Tree's ultimate goal is to mitigate the seemingly unchangeable problem of unequal distribution of education resources. We take social responsibility as the very core of our operation.

"Online"

Internet plays an exclusively essential role in the 21st century, to we recognise it as the best way to create real impact and brought opportunity to those who are desperately needed. Considering that internet access in Chinese suburban is quite accessible, internet can be a way to go.

As shown above, The Glowing Tree incorporate two major components.

First, is a Free Online Education Platform. Being the non-profitable part of the programme, the website will serve as a "Chinese version of the Khan Academy™️". The goal of the website, as introduced, is to provide opportunity to students that have no access to high school education and are longing for such. As mentioned in our research, such area has long been desolated and needs to be addressed by proper solution.

The website, at its initial stage, will provides three of the major courses in Chinese High School Curriculum. The team will also seise to cooperate with some of the most established free online teaching platform for the opportunity to reprint or translate the existed resources. At the same time, the team will develop a range of thorough learning structures referring to the traditional high school diploma and related skills that may be required. Then, the team will utilise the structure to sort the resources into different learning stages to create learning plans for students, making it much more easy for students to follow.

Second, is a profitable online one-to-one tutoring platform.

As mentioned in the status quo, the Chinese off-campus education market has been a real mess. Therefore, what the Glowing Tree tutoring programme is trying to achieve is to provide an affordable online tutoring programme that can be especially helpful for those who are not financially superior. To make this possible, the Glowing Tree team cooperates with a range of kind and knowledgable college students and graduate students as a tutor.

We are currently putting all we got in this programme and hopefully, it will mark the start of the whole programme.
