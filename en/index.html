About Us

The Glowing Tree is a hybrid education programme created by a young team from China dedicated to pay its humble effort to one of the greatest social problem in China, namely, the distribution in education resources. Creating in 2018, The team wants to take its unique concept through the eyes of the public, seeking for examination and improvement, aiming to pay its contribution to the problem. Recognising the limitation in its ability, the team not only seise to materialise the programme, but also to raise the public awareness of the issue and possible solution. As suggested by the name of the programme, by the same time when we are plant our glowing tree, we expect more glowing trees will be planted aside ours. Together, hopefully, we will light a path for these young talented children.
